Prawn Fried Rice

A Quick Prawn Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp frozen chopped garlic
  • 1 tsp frozen chopped ginger
  • ½ red chilli, sliced, optional
  • 80g frozen shredded Savoy cabbage
  • 1 medium carrot, grated
  • 150g leftover cooked rice
  • 1 tsp reduced-salt soy sauce
  • ½ tsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large frying pan or wok. Stir-fry the garlic, ginger and chilli (if using), together for 1 min over high heat, until fragrant.
  2. Add the frozen cabbage and the grated carrot and cook for 2-3 mins until softened.
  3. Add the rice to the pan, along with the sesame oil and soy sauce and cook for 5 mins, stirring constantly, until beginning to crisp.
  4. Add the spring onion and the prawns and cook for 1 min to heat through.
  5. Spoon the fried rice onto a plate and drizzle with the sriracha sauce and some extra chopped chilli if you like.
